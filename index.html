About SAC

The Student Activity Council (SAC) is a vital component of college life, comprising elected and volunteer students working in collaboration with a faculty advisor. Guided by a constitution or bylaws, this council serves as a structured platform for student expression and assistance in college affairs and activities. It not only fosters opportunities for leadership experience but also actively encourages student involvement, creating a vibrant and engaged campus community. With a focus on democratic processes and mentorship from a faculty advisor, the SAC plays a pivotal role in shaping the college experience by providing a means for students to voice their opinions, contribute to decision-making, and contribute to the overall growth and development of the college community.
